Nigerian Yam Porridge Recipe

Description

Yam pottage/Yam Porridge (Asaro) Recipe: Asaro is a very delicious yam recipe. Although it is eaten by most tribes in Nigeria, it is more common in the Yoruba-speaking parts of Nigeria. It is also easy to make. If you love yam, this recipe is a must-try. You won't regret it!

Ingredients

  • African yam
  • Red bell pepper
  • Tomato
  • Habanero pepper
  • Palm oil
  • Onion
  • Salt & Seasoning
  • Fish & crayfish
  • Spinach or Ugwu leaves

Steps

  1. Peel and cut and rinse the yam
  2. Blend your pepper mix
  3. Cook the yam and the pepper mix together
  4. Stir in the Palm oil, sliced onions, and crayfish
  5. Mash the yams
  6. Add the fish
  7. Leave to simmer
  8. Stir in your delish veggies
  9. Serve and let it be sweet in your mouth
